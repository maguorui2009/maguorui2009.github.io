About me

I obtained my B.S. degree (Honor Track) at the college of mathematics, Sichuan University, Chengdu, China, in 2019. I am currently pursuing the Ph. D. degree in Applied Mathematics from the Department of Mathematical Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Stephen, Yau, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China.

Research interests

My current research interest lies in the statistical signal processing problems related to machine learning, especially filtering and estimation problems based on state-space models (SSMs).
